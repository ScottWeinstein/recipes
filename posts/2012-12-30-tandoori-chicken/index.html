Tandoori chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken breasts
  • 1.5 bulbs garlic
  • 1.5" (1" round) ginger, peeled
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt or 1/2 c buttermilk
  • 1/8 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup methi leaves (kasuri)
  • 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper or to taste
  • 1/4 cup Shan tandoori powder (about half the packet)
  • 1 tsp Shan Zafrani garam masala
  • 1 tsp chunky chat masala (any brand) optional

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into large cubes
  2. Blend together garlic, ginger, vinegar, oil, yogurt, lemon juice, methi, salt, chilli, tandoori powder, garam masala
  3. Marinate chicken overnight with 1/3 of the mixture
  4. Grill on hot coals, or broil (25 min)
  5. Baste occasionally with ghee, and sprinkle chat masala when done.
  6. Serve with raw sliced onions and lemon wedges
